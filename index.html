The C.F. Barker Archives

Genealogical research about the ancestors of Ralph and Nyria Denton-Barker and the descendants of their grandparents (the father of one of whom was C. F. Barker).

This is the homepage for our family archives. From here, you can download the tree (which is a single A3 sheet displaying everyone) and the book (which is an A4 format suitable for printing).

The most recent version is from 9 October 2018.

Also, all photographs (some of which appear in the book above) are online in our Flickr group and all source documents in our Zenodo community.

We invite you to contribute to the Archives via Github, Flickr, or Zenodo.

For more information, contact sam@samwilson.id.au.
